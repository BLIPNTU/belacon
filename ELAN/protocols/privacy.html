Complete detailed account of what we do to protect privacy is to be found under the Data Policy Guide section of the wiki.

With regards to your day-to-day transcription:

  • When the baby’s name is mentioned, transcribe it as BABYNAME.
  • Whenever any specific name is mentioned in the file, transcribe it as ADULTNAME.
  • For sibling’s name, indicate as SIBLINGNAME.
  • When transcribing Video Call Story Time recordings from the Talk-Together Study, any mention of a participant’s Talk Together ID (also known as their Qualtrics ID) should be transcribed as QUALTRICSID. The ID consists of 5 digits (e.g., 12345).
  • If you encounter an instance that you’re not sure how to handle, you may use the following code to comment and mark the potentially protected stretch of audio: :PROTECTION-CHECK. Do inform your supervisor if you utilise this so that they can check that section.

Check the Data Policy Guide for what constitutes protected data. Check the Frequently Asked Questions should you have further questions on how to tag and mark protected data.
